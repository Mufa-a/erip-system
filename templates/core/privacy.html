Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide when creating an account, such as your name, email address, and business details. We also collect usage data to improve the platform.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to provide and improve the ERIP service, send important account notifications, and process payments. We do not sell your data to third parties.

3. Data Security

All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We use industry-standard security practices to protect your business information.

4. M-Pesa Transactions

M-Pesa transaction data is processed through Safaricom's Daraja API. We store transaction records for your accounting purposes but do not store M-Pesa PINs or sensitive payment credentials.

5. Contact

For privacy concerns, contact us at privacy@eripsystem.co.ke.
