Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start my free trial?
Click "Start free trial" on any page. Create your account — no credit card needed. You get 14 days full access.
How does M-Pesa integration work?
We use the Safaricom Daraja API. Once you enter your Paybill or Till number, customers can pay via STK push and invoices update automatically.
Can I switch plans later?
Yes. You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time from your account settings. Changes take effect on your next billing cycle.
Is my data safe?
Yes. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We back up your data daily and store it securely in Kenyan-based servers.
